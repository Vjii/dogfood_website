What should dogs eat?

At PetPlate, we believe your dog is meant to eat real food, made from natural ingredients — every bit as natural as what you eat. We’ve worked with veterinarians from Cornell to develop a complete and balanced menu designed to improve the health and well-being of your pet, using only ingredients fit for the family table. Our homemade meals are cooked fresh every day in Brooklyn and delivered to your door within 24 hours. Easy, convenient, healthy. The right food will improve your dog’s overall health and happiness.
